THIS IS HOW I ORGANIZE MY PHOTOS

I FIRST UPLOAD EVERYTHING ONTO GOOGLE DRIVE BECAUSE I DON'T TRUST MYSELF WITH A PHYSICAL HARD DRIVE.

AND THEN I CATEGORIZE EVERYTHING INTO YEARS.

THE YEARS ARE SEPARATED INTO MONTHS AND I ALSO HAVE SPECIFIC FOLDERS MADE FOR MEMORABLE EVENTS AND PHOTO FILES THAT MAY COME IN HANDY. FOR EXAMPLE, I TAKE A LOT OF SCREENSHOTS OF IMPORTANT INFORMATION SO I KEEP THEM SAVED IN A SEPARATE FILE.

ORGANIZING THINGS INTO MONTHS WORKS REALLY WELL FOR ME BECAUSE IT HELPS ME REKINDLE MEMORIES IN A CHRONOLOGICAL ORDER. FOR EXAMPLE, THE JANUARY FOLDER ALWAYS STARTS OFF WITH NYE PARTY PHOTOS AND AFTER BEING REMINDED OF THIS EVENT IT HELPS ME SLOWLY REMEMBER OTHER THINGS THAT I DID CLOSE TO THIS DATE.
